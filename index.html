Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection & Privacy

This application is designed as a localized network management tool. It performs Layer 2 and Layer 3 discovery on your local area network (LAN) to identify and configure IP surveillance equipment.

  • No Cloud Transmission: No IP addresses, MAC addresses, device credentials, or network topology data are ever transmitted to the developer, third-party servers, or external analytics services.
  • Local Processing Only: All discovery, configuration, and logging happen entirely on your local machine.

2. Subscription & Billing

Access to the application's core features requires an active auto-renewing subscription.

  • Billing is handled entirely securely by Microsoft (for Windows users) or Apple (for macOS users).
  • Subscriptions auto-renew annually unless canceled in your respective Store Account Settings at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

3. Liability Limitations

This software interacts directly with the network interfaces of hardware manufactured by third parties (Hikvision, Dahua, Axis, ONVIF-compliant devices, etc.). The developer is not liable for device unresponsiveness, IP conflicts, or network downtime resulting from user-initiated mass-configuration tasks.

4. Contact & Support

If you require technical assistance or have questions regarding your subscription, please manage your billing through your native OS App Store settings.
